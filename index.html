About

KLE’s College of BCA, RLS Institute welcomes you to the gala of jamboree. Here, you get a glimpse of all the events that are excited for your arrival. Carpediem is waiting for you to freeze the stance of fashion. A rave of paradise that will make you move on your tippy toes to the beat of the Rhythm. The illusions affair has a lot of games to offer. The quest within has hidden a secret for you to find. The stamp is missing on the hatter’s mark and is waiting for you to discover it. The artsy masquerade is joyous for you to explore your artistic mind. The wizard of glory is on the seat and is ready to question your knowledge. The matrix nirvana has a bundle of goods waiting for you to sell. The heat of winter snow want to hear you defend what’s right. The elite cathedra is waiting to hand over its title. What are you waiting for, come join us in the world of CARNIVAL.
